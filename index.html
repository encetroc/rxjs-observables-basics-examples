Subject

  • replay subject re-emits all the values of the subject or a portion of them
  • behaviour subject emits all upcoming and the last previous value
  • normal subject emit just upcoming values

    Cold vs hot observables

    cold observables start emiting only if you subscribe to it, and stops emiting when you unsubscribe.

    hot observables are always emiting regardless of if you are subscribed or not. beware that a hot observable continues emitting thoughout its life anf if want to stop it you will have to do so manually
